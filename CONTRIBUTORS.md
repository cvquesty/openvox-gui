## How to Contribute

We welcome contributions of all kinds:

- 🐛 **Bug reports** — Found something broken? [Open an issue](https://github.com/cvquesty/openvox-gui/issues)
- 💡 **Feature requests** — Have an idea? Let us know
- 🧪 **Testing** — Try it out and tell us what works (and what doesn't)
- 📖 **Documentation** — Improvements, corrections, and translations
- 🔧 **Code** — Pull requests are welcome

See the project [README](README.md) for setup instructions.
